5-Star Rated Movies

Movie: Twilight

It is an escape movie where the worlds of humans, vampires and werewolves unites.

Series: Just Add Water, H2O

It is a series with seasons about 3 high school girls who discovers that they are mermaids and avoid at all cost to touch water in public.

I fell in love with this movie when I was a kid and the graphics of this series is very good.
If you like watching a series with a lot of tension and powers - I recommend this series for you!

Movie: The Lion King (real life version)

This movie is about a young cub finding is identity in his loving and caring father, but tradgedy stikes Mufasa, the father, which leads to Simba, the cub, to go in exile and run from his past.

I recommend this movie, because of its lessons and becuase it will make you cry both sad tears and happy tears!
